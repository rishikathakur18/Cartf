Cartfolio!

Cartfolio is an Indian fashion e-commerce company headquartered in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India. The company was founded in 2007-2008 to sell personalized gift items.[2][3][4][5] In May 2014, Cartfolio.com was acquired by Myntra. In May 2022, Cartfolio launched an express delivery service on its app to offer a one-of-a-kind experience on its fashion and beauty platform. This service allows shoppers to receive their orders for products marked with ‘M-express tag’ on the listing page within 24–48 hours of purchase.
